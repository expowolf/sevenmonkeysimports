The Collection

Folk art that makes a room smile.

Every piece is handmade and chosen with care. Inventory changes constantly — these are just a few of our favorites. Stop in to see what's new.

Sculptures

Hand-welded metal figures, carved wood, and ceramic creatures. Showstoppers for shelves, gardens, and entryways.

Pottery & Barro Negro

Burnished black clay from Oaxaca, talavera, candle holders, and traditional vessels.

Mosaic Decor

Hand-tiled mushrooms, mirrors, and accents that catch the light in every color of the rainbow.

Catrinas & Day of the Dead

Iconic hand-painted Catrina figurines with fabric dresses and feathered hats. Each one is a little masterpiece.

Garden Art

Whimsical metal critters built to live outside — ants, dragonflies, butterflies, owls, and more.

Jewelry & Beadwork

Huichol beaded jewelry, woven bracelets, and one-of-a-kind earrings made by hand.

Wall Art

Punched tin mirrors, painted retablos, woven tapestries, and bright folk paintings.

Handbags & Textiles

Woven bags, embroidered blouses, blankets, and table runners — every thread a story.

Toys & Alebrijes

Carved and painted wooden creatures, spinning tops, and folk toys for collectors and kids alike.
