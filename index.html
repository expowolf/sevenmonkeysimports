Our Story

Run by Jane & Jim — with a whole lot of heart.

Seven Monkey Imports began as a dream: a happy, colorful place where the work of Latin America's most talented artisans could find new homes across Door County and beyond. Every piece in the shop is chosen with care, sourced directly, and priced so you can actually take it home.

Stop by, say hi, and let the colors do the rest.

The Collection

Folk art that makes a room smile.

Sculptures

Hand-welded metal figures, carved wood, and ceramic creatures. Showstoppers for shelves, gardens, and entryways.

Pottery & Barro Negro

Burnished black clay from Oaxaca, talavera, and traditional folk pottery — vases, candle holders, and vessels.

Wall Art

Punched tin mirrors, painted retablos, woven tapestries, and bright Mexican folk paintings.

Handbags & Textiles

Woven bags, embroidered blouses, blankets, and runners — every thread a story.

Toys & Alebrijes

Carved and painted wooden creatures, spinning tops, and folk toys for collectors and kids alike.

Jewelry Boxes & Gifts

Inlaid wood, painted tin, and one-of-a-kind keepsakes you won't find anywhere else.
