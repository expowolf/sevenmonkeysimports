Our Story

Run by Jane & Jim — with a whole lot of heart.

Seven Monkey Imports began as a dream: a happy, colorful place where the work of Latin America's most talented artisans could find new homes across Door County and beyond.

Every piece in the shop is chosen with care, sourced directly from the makers, and priced so you can actually take it home. We travel, we visit workshops, and we bring back pieces with real stories behind them — burnished black clay from Oaxaca, beadwork from the Huichol, hand-welded garden critters, Catrinas, mosaic mushrooms, woven textiles, and so much more.

What makes us happiest is seeing a piece find its person. Stop by, say hi, and let the colors do the rest.
